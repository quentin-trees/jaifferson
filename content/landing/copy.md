## Section 2 — What's in a Jaifferson profile

**H2**: This isn't a LinkedIn summary. It's a decoded human.

2. **Compressed CV** — Signal over noise. The moves that matter.
3. **Deep values** — What they optimize for when nobody's watching.
4. **MBTI hypothesis** — Cognitive style, with reasoning — not a quiz result.
5. **Psychological profile** — Likely drivers, probable wounds, decision patterns.
6. **What wasn't said** — The silences that carry signal.
7. **Blue ocean** — The opportunity they're uniquely positioned to own.
8. **Fit analysis** — How they map to you, your company, or your opening.
9. **Approach strategy** — The exact angle, message, and timing to land the meeting.
10. **One-line portrait** — The final punch.
